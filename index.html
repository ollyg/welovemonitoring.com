The Podcast

We Love Monitoring covers news and stories about application and infrastructure monitoring. You use monitoring every day. Stay up-to-date and listen to tool authors and practitioners like you as they tell their monitoring stories.

The world is a better place when monitoring does not suck.

Want to Be on the Podcast?

Do you have a unique perspective on monitoring? Did you write your own monitoring tool? Is your monitoring setup a labor of love? Do you hate monitoring? The world needs to hear your voice. Tell us why
